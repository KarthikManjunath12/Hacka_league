RULES
• Teams can consist of 2-4 members.
• All Peripheral Hardware must be approved by the organisers.
• All design elements, code and hardware must be prepared on- spot.
• Intellectual Property developed should belong to the Participants.
• Preliminary round consists of pseudocodes and hands on coding questions based on data structures and simple algorithms.
• Second round is the Ideation phase of a solution to real world industrial problems.
• The Final Round is a 12 hour Hackathon based towards developing world industrial working model/simulation
  of the solution brought about in the ideation phase.
• All participants will be provided with a participation certificate.
• The Winners and the Runners-up shall receive Exciting Prizes.
Breakfast, Lunch,Dinner and High Tea will be provided at the venue.
